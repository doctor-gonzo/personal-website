Core Ideas / Thesis

  • Technical alignment work – including ELK, contrast-consistent search (CCS), tuned-lens methods, concept erasure, model fingerprinting, mechanistic interpretability, scalable oversight, constitutional AI, etc. – is promising and important, but we should likely pay just as much attention to the geopolitical, economic, and societal "rapid transition" aspect of AI progress, and the “information overwhelm” aspect of trying to construct / debate good AI policy (+ desired futures)
  • A considerable portion of the existential / “bad futures” risk we face comes from unstable equilibria / misaligned incentives (and/or "arms race" dynamics) which currently exist within and between different actors / forces: AI labs, countries, OSS
  • We need to proactively create specific tools to align the incentives of as many countries (and people) as possible, and to help "normal" people express opinions / guide policy / track information / earn money from AI (potentially via "RLHF Union") / understand what is happening in real-time, as "shared reality" increasingly threatens to disintegrate
  • We need a “living database” of all of these that you can converse with via personalised time-saving metaphors and memes and via “social AI games”
  • Ethereum is an ideal “AI Alignment Substrate” / platform for “Social Infrastructure” (+for managing access to models in an auditable way +for decision-chain transparency and integrity +for enabling self-enforcing treaties), and ZK/FHE gives us a lot of options when it comes to “privacy-preserving surveillance" of GPUs / training runs / LLM use 
  • One of the key urgencies of our time is accelerating the adoption of on-chain identity / anti-sybil primitives / public-key infrastructure and making these accessible to non-technical people + legible for policymakers (as a building block). The best way to do this is likely through novel (and fun) interfaces/trials which are “game-like” / futuristic "public-good social-media" (+which need to leverage the latest EIP updates to be feasible – see below)
  • There probably exist policies (in unexplored design-space recently unlocked by advances in ZK cryptography + related tech) which would satisfy each of the “AI doomer”, "EA / longtermist", and "OSS / accelerationist" camps simultaneously
