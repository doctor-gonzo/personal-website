Zuzalu Presentation (2023)

Talk context for Social Infrastructure for AI on Ethereum and the Context Engine AI safety ideas map.

Event | Open full presentation page

Thesis

Ethereum can be public infrastructure for AI-era coordination: records, commitments, permissions, and incentives that humans and agents can inspect.

Risk surface

More capable agents raise the stakes for identity, delegation, provenance, dispute resolution, and economic feedback loops.

Crypto tools

Signatures, attestations, encrypted records, token-gated access, onchain commitments, quadratic mechanisms, and public audit trails.
