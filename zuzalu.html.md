# Zuzalu AI x Crypto

Charlie Thompson's 2023 talk, "Social Infrastructure for AI on Ethereum," with an embedded Context Engine AI safety ideas map.

## Argument map

Static guide to the exported notes:

- Thesis: Ethereum can act as public infrastructure for AI-era coordination through records, commitments, incentives, and permissions that humans and agents can both inspect.
- Risk surface: More capable agents raise the stakes for identity, delegation, provenance, dispute resolution, and economic feedback loops.
- Crypto tools: Signatures, attestations, encrypted records, token-gated access, onchain commitments, quadratic mechanisms, and public audit trails.
- Open questions: What should be public, what should be private, who can update the map, how are summaries challenged, and how do agents inherit human intent without hiding the decision process?
